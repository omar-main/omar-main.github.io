## What This Blog Is

This is where I document my journey through offensive security — CTF challenges, hands-on pentesting labs, networking deep-dives, and anything else I find worth writing up.

If you found a write-up here from a CTF or HackTheBox box, welcome. If you're here from my GitHub, also welcome.

## What to Expect

- **CTF Write-ups** — post-CTF breakdowns of challenges I solved (and some I didn't)
- **Lab Notes** — HackTheBox, TryHackMe, and self-hosted lab walkthroughs
- **Tool Deep-dives** — Burp Suite, Wireshark, Nmap, and others
- **Research** — anything interesting I stumble across in web app security, network security, or reverse engineering

## Background

I'm currently studying **BSc Computer Science (Cybersecurity)** at Asia Pacific University (APU), with a dual award from De Montfort University. I hold a CCNA and spend most of my free time breaking things in controlled environments.

Recent highlight: **26th place** at Bingo CTF 2025 with team *dehashed*.
